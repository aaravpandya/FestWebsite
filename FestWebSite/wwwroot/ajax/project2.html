HackBMU

Description

HackBMU is a hackathon focused on promoting innovation and entrepreneurship and enabling networking among all future hackers. We will host upcoming engineers and developers from all over India to create mobile, web, and hardware hacks.

‍ Last year, HackBMU 1.0 garnered huge attention with 20+ teams from around the country. Also, we aimed to close the gender gap in hackathon participation and encouraged female participants to join up by reserving seats. The response was overwhelming, and nearly half of our participants were women.

‍ Riding on the success of last year’s event, we’d like to aim for even greater heights. The previous version of HackBMU was a 36-hour event. This time around, we are planning to extend the time period to 48 hours.

sci-ex exhibit

Description

“The true essence of an engineer is exploration and experimentation”. To fulfill this, the SATA club, BMU is organizing Sci-ex, a science exhibition at the 67th Milestone. The main aim of this science exhibit is to provide students a platform for exhibiting their projects and ideas.

The event is aimed at creating enthusiasm among students to learn and work on new concepts. The event will provide a platform for aspiring scientists and engineers of near future to present their creative and innovative engineering solutions and prototypes. The event will provide an opportunity to interact with fellow innovators and exchange ideas. All science and engineering students from schools/ universities/colleges are welcome to participate in this exciting competition and win exciting prizes apart from having fun.

Description

line follower

Description

The line follower robot is a mobile machine that can detect and follow a predefined path marked by a black line on a white surface with high contrasted color. Participants must complete the paths as per rules and regulations. The competing line follower which can successfully face all the challenges and find its way to the finish point in the least amount of time emerges to be the winner. This is a competition where time is everything and the machine’s efficiency is to be proven.
