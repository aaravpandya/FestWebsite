model united nations

Description

Model United Nations, also known as Model UN or MUN, is an educational simulation and/or academic activity in which students can learn about diplomacy, international relations, and the United Nations. MUN involves and teaches participants speaking, debating, and writing skills, in addition to critical thinking, teamwork, and leadership abilities.

‍ Participants of MUN are known as delegates, who represent the view point of a country on a global issue.

‍ MUN is a unique in itself form of devate, where not only the way you talk matters but also what you talk about. It gives the participants a chance to assume roles of international leaders and delve into solving complex problems. “This is our world, a common world, everyone should feel a common responsibility” -Ban-ki Moon, former Secretary General, United Nations. A MUN is just a step towards that realization

Era of Trade

Description

Trade exists due to specialization and the division of labour, a predominant form of economic activity in which individuals and groups concentrate on a small aspect of production, but use their output in trades for other products and needs. The most common medium of exchange for these transactions is money. In Enactus, we believe that every individual has the ability to make impact in their community - not in the form of charity - but by using the positive power of business. So, here we, ENACTUS BMU welcomes all those who can deal well with money, business or trades to the “ERA TO TRADE” which is a competition to check your trading ability.

This is a two-day event, where the participants will be given certain tasks. Upon successful completion, they will be given certain companies for which the need to present business model or should provide a solution to any real-life problem of the firm.

To make this competition rewarding, the winners would be awarded with cash prizes and vouchers. It will be a team competition, with a team of 4 members.
