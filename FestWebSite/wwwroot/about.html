the 67th Milestone

About

67th Milestone ‘19 is the third annual Techno-Cultural and Management fest of BMU. 67th Milestone is a multi-faceted festival which will host a wide range of cultural and technical programs and this edition of the fest is to be hosted from from 8th-10th February 2019.

Throughout the three days, it will offer a platform for artists to perform, techies to prove their brilliance, speed enthusiasts to get behind the wheel, and fashion freaks to walk the ramp. With the 67th Milestone only a few months away, every corner of the campus on eavesdropping has some gossip about 67th Milestone to offer. Observing the success of previous editions of the ‘67th Milestone’, with a huge participation from various colleges and universities, the 67th Milestone ‘19 will strive to surp benchmarks for the Annual fests in India. 67th Milestone uniquely distinguishes itself to their partners by offering companies an opportunity to hold recruitment rounds for students. Students, as well as every other individual present for this promising event, will have the opportunity to witness performing arts at it’s best, technical geniuses battling against each other for the throne, and to gain inspiration from and interact with eminent leaders of the industry, executives from leading business houses and achievers from their respective spheres.

Rewind 2018

67th Milestone is a multi-faceted event that will host a broad spectrum of activities. Throughout the three days, it will offer a platform for artists to perform, techies to prove their brilliance, speed enthusiasts to get behind the wheels, and fashion freaks to walk the ramp.

With over 3000 students participating, the previous two editions of 67th Milestone had some of the most successful events and star nights like EDM Night by USAI (Italian Disc Jockey), concert by The Parikrama Band, an EDM night by Sunburn, concert by Babbal Rai – a well-recognized and renowned Punjabi singer, an entrepreneurship talk by Mr. Kunal Kapoor - CEO Ketto & Bollywood actor, The Ecokart, Comedy Toll Plaza (Jaspreet, Nishant & Nitin), Battle of Bands, Hackathon, Theatre Phantamonica, and Beat the Street
